What is Youtube?

YouTube is an online platform where people can watch, create, and share videos on almost any topic, from entertainment and gaming to education and news. Launched in 2005 and later acquired by Google, it has grown into one of the largest websites in the world, allowing creators to upload content and build audiences through subscriptions and recommendations. Users can interact with videos by liking, commenting, and sharing them, while creators can earn money through ads, memberships, and sponsorships. Overall, YouTube serves as both a major source of entertainment and a powerful tool for communication and learning across the globe.
